## 2024-05-18 - Tooltips and ARIA Labels for Icon-Only Buttons

**Learning:** Icon-only buttons (like ✎ and 🗑) can be confusing for screen reader users and those who might not recognize the icon. It's a common accessibility and usability issue.
**Action:** Always add `aria-label` to provide screen readers with context, and `title` to show a tooltip on hover for visual users, ensuring everyone understands the button's action.
